About

Hello stranger, I am Kei'ichiro, "Kei'ichi" for short, "Kei" for shorter. Pleased to meet you.

I strive to create memorable shared stories and experiences thorugh utilizing both design thinking fundamentals and game design thoery to create innovative solutions.
Specialties: User Experience Design, User Research, Information Architechture, Storybuilding, Multiplayer Experiences, Game Design, Video Editing.

Current resume can be found here.

I studied User Experience Design and Information Systems at the University of Toronto Faculty of Information Masters Program between 2015-2017. My undergraduate studies consisted of an Honors Bachelor of Arts at the University of Toronto between 2011-2015 and a Certificate in Digital Media from Sheridan College between 2013-2015. That sure was a whole lot of school.

On the side I enjoy playing video games, translating media from JPN->ENG, hiking, and travelling for various events and experiences.
